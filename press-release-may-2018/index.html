Early Learning Suite Formal Release

WASHINGTON D.C., May 21, 2018 -- Netsource Interactive, Inc. announced today the formal release of the Early Learning Suite. The Early Learning Suite is the only integrated platform that supports Early Childhood Education. This platform is highly customizable to meet the unique needs of each state or jurisdiction.

“We have been working on this for the last eighteen months and are excited to release this product suite” said Issa Barkett, President of Netsource Interactive, Inc.

The following are some highlights of the product suite:

Licensing & Compliance: This module provides a platform for performing the licensing and compliance function. This module was developed with a specific focus on providing the center owners with an easy to use set-by-step electronic workflow. This includes orientation material, information about required documents, and tools to help track and schedule the various licensing visits. This module provides the licensing and compliance team with a more productive platform while not sacrificing the tracking and audit requirements.

Professional Development: This module provides a platform for the tracking of professional development for early learning professionals (Registry). It also manages both classroom-based and online eLearning courses.

Subsidy Module: A user friendly module that allows caseworkers and childcare centers the ability to process applications and renewals for subsidized childcare. This includes eligibility determination and an electronic case folder with required eligibility documents. This module also can handle family co-payments, renewals and appeals.

Attendance Module: Includes a mobile friendly web application that allows parents or guardians the ability to mark attendance (Check-in and Check-out) with a confirmation from the childcare centers. This enables payments to providers and tools to integrate with state-based financial systems.

In addition to the above modules, this platform can provide information to a parent's portal with updated information from center directors (via the Director Portal) and QRIS data on each center.
